Welcome to Blomdaele

This magnificent listed building is located in the historic centre of Bruges, between the 't Zand and the market in a peaceful location.
It is the ideal base to discover the most important museums or the Bruges countryside.

The building dating from 1897 served for many years as an office for the tax department.
In 2012-2013 the house was completely renovated with respect for the old elements combined with all modern comfort of today.

The house offers two luxurious guest rooms and is equipped with a lift.
There is the possibility to park your car for €20 per day in our garage.

In the attic you can use our fitness and sauna area. (sauna or infrared cabin at an additional cost)

Between the suites on the first floor there is a coffee and tea area (free of charge). Various beers and soft drinks are also available (at an additional cost).

Children are welcome from the age of 10.

Pets are not allowed.

The entire house is smoke-free!

From 01/01/15 the city of Bruges has decided to introduce a tourist tax of €3 per person per night. The tax only applies to persons over 18 years of age.
